Entrepreneurship is the process of starting a business or other organization. You have to develop a plan about how many workers and how much money you need, whom you will sell, etc.Once you are ready with a business plan, you can take monetary help from banks or govt. agencies like NIF.Therefore, every village adult should have a bank account. Do NOT borrow money from moneylenders.
Several people in the village can come together and pool in their resources to start an enterprise.

1.Knitting and Stitching

You would require good tailors, few sewing machines and some other stuff like thread etc.Initially you do not have to hire anyone as you could start alone and wait for customers to come.

2. Food Catering and Packaging

Next great thing that a woman can excel is the catering of food and beverages.If you live on the outskirts of some rural area then you could take orders & start a Tiffin services.You can cater to the people who work from 9 to 5. If your food is good at right price then you might get orders for thousands customers daily.

4. Papad/Pickle making

Men and women of the village can come together and start a papad-making company. You can take orders from vendors and sellers in the the nearby town.
