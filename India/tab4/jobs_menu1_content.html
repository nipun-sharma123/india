Vocational Courses

Government has started several programmes to teach rural Indians skills like Tailoring, Data entry operating, Typewriting,etc. After doing these courses job with a minimum wage is guaranteed. There are several schemes like Aajeevika skills, National skill development programme, National Vocational Training Institutes, etc. offering vocational training.
