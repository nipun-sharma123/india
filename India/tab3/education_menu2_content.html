4. HUMANITIES - Stream with all the humanities courses.

Polytechnic Exam-

The Diploma in Engineering or Diploma in Technical Studies is a technical degree below the undergraduate rank which aims to provide students with some basic knowledge of engineering

Staff Selection Commission (SSC)

SSC conducts clerks grade examination for those who passed matriculation.

Indian Army/Navy,

After matriculation, one can join in Indian Army/Navy to the posts of soldiers in technical trade through written exams.

Police Force :

On the basis of physical test and written test, one can join as constable (tradesmen) in Central Reserve Police Force (CRPF).
