Pregnancy and child Birth


About : Morning sickness, constipation, tiredness are common. Don’t worry.
Recommended Diet
Proteins:Include dals, pulses, sprouts, eggs, meats, fish, milk and milk products.

Carbohydrates:Wheat (properly cooked roti), whole fruits and salads

Fats:Add Alsi, Akhrot, beans, olive and soyabean oil to your diet. You can take cod liver oil capsules available in nearby swasthya seva kendra.

Iron:Green leafy vegetables, beans and pulses, pomegranate, raisins, dates. Vitamin C is essential for the absorption of iron - squeeze a lemon in your meal.

Calcium:Consume milk and milk products in various forms, nuts, seeds, beans, green leafy vegetables and seafood.
Foods to avoid

THING TO DO:

• Do not have fluids like water or juices the first thing in the morning. Start your day with a toast or biscuits.

• Keep yourself hydrated throughout the day.Drink minimum 8 glasses of water per day.

• Avoid over eating and have six small balanced meals throughout the day.

• Wash all food items thoroughly before eating.

• Consult your local sevika in Swasthya seva Kendra before taking any kind of medicine even if it is for headache or cold or cough.

• Avoid having X-ray , even if local doctor asks you to.

• Avoid getting into contact with pesticides and fertilisers in any form. The chemicals are extremely harmful.

• Not more than two cups of tea/coffee, raw seafood, street food, undercooked food.Don’t eat papya and pineapple.

• Take rest.Sleep for mimimum eght hours. Family members and neighbours should take care that a pregnant woman doesn’t have to do tiresome household chores like drawing water from the well. Help her as much as possible.

• If the pain increases much do not take normal pain killers.Family members should immediately call the local helpline no.

• Go to the policy section and see the Janani Shishu Suraksha Yojana(JSY)
