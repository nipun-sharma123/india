Sanitation and Hygiene

1.Soap and wood ash are both cleansing and disinfecting agents when used with water and can be used to kill pathogens on hands and utensils.The most important times that hands should be washed with soap and water are:
• After defecating.

• After cleaning a child who has defecated.

• Before eating or handling food.

Hand washing with soap is among the most effective and inexpensive ways to prevent diarrhoeal diseases and pneumonia.

2.If you have an infected cut, burn or wound on your hand, do not work in food preparation.

3.Wear washed clothes daily and also wash their undergarments everyday.

4.Clean ears with cotton bud regularly and put hanky on your mouth when coughing. wash eyes with water regularly to avoid eye infections.

5.Do not eat tobacco,smoke or drink.

6.Keep water storing vessels covered and clean at all times.

7.Cover food articles so that mosquitoes and flies don’t containate them.

8. Keep surroundings clean, dispose waste properly and do not let water stagnate.

9. Wash fruits and vegetables before direct consumption or cooking.

10.Don’t defecate in the open.
