Family Planning

India is the second most populous country of the world.The GoI’s initiative of ‘Hum do Hamare do’ is the necessity of today. If you give birth to more no of children, they become a liability.You have to feed them, clothe them and educate them which is not affordable. Already India faces the situation of ‘ek anaar sau bimaar’. Therefore, proper family planning is the need of today.
What to do:
• Make use of contraceptive method to prevent unwanted childbirths. Remember ‘Hum do, hamare do’.
• Marrying a girl before 18 years of age and a boy before 21 years is illegal in our country. If you know anyone in your family or friends who is being forced at early marriage, make them aware about this law. If they didn’t agree, don’t hesitate to file a case against them.
• There should be a healthy spacing of 3 years as it improves the chances of survival of infants and also helps in reducing the impact of population momentum on population growth. Don’t hurry up.
• Male cititzens should not hesitate in playing their part,They should participate in the free camps organized by the govt. for non-scalpel vasectomy.
